EZBIKE

The easiest way to get from a to b on a citibike

ABOUT
We made this after being frustrated with what Citibike offered online. The app has so much going on that it doesn't seem to work at all most of the time.

We wanted something SIMPLE that would tell us where to find a bike where we were and where to find an open dock at our destiniation.

Still very much in progress so check us out and fork us on Github. Looks OK on a phone so bookmark to your homepage.
